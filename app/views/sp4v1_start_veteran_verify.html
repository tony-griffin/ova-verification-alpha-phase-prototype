Apply for a veteran’s ID card

Use this service to apply for a card that you can use to identify yourself as a veteran of the UK armed forces.

The card lets you access services and benefits for veterans.

What you need to apply

  • your service number
  • the branch of service you served with most recently
  • the year you enlisted
  • the year you left
  • your National Insurance number

You’ll also be asked to upload a passport-style photo.
