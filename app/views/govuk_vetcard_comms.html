What sort of communications will I be sent

We send email and SMS (short message service) text messages to veterans using GOV.UK Notify.

These could include updates about the support that veterans are eligible for, information about the wider veteran community and other relevant information for UK ex-service personnel.

You can opt out of receiving communications at any time.
